Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We understand that you might have general inquiries before reaching out. To assist with common questions, we have put together a list of frequently asked questions related to buying, selling, and using our platform. Please check below for some helpful information that may answer your questions quickly:

  • How do I list my car for sale?

    You can easily list your car on our platform by creating an account and filling out the details of your vehicle. Make sure to upload clear photos and provide an accurate description to attract potential buyers. We also offer tips on pricing your car competitively.

  • How do I search for cars?

    To find the perfect second-hand car, use the advanced search filters on our website. You can filter by make, model, year, price range, mileage, and more. We also offer car recommendations based on your preferences.

  • Can I schedule a test drive?

    Yes! Once you find a car you're interested in, contact the seller to arrange a test drive. We always recommend you inspect the car thoroughly before making any decisions.

  • What should I do if I’m having trouble navigating the site?

    If you encounter any issues while using the website or face any technical difficulties, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team. We will help you resolve the problem quickly.

  • What payment methods are accepted on your platform?

    We support a variety of secure payment methods, and you can choose the one that works best for you. However, we recommend using secure payment channels for the safety of both the buyer and seller.
